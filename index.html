Welcome to Ulster County Recreation Map

Ulster County is rich in recreational resources. In fact, roughly one-third of the lands and waters of Ulster County are publicly-accessible for recreation. Ulster County REConnect includes the lands and waters of Ulster County that are open for enjoyment and exploration by the public, and much, much more. For the first time ever, all of this information is available online - in one place, and free of charge.

The purpose of this interactive mapping tool is to strengthen the connection between Ulster County residents and visitors to the fabulous recreational opportunities that exist throughout Ulster County, NY. It is our hope that Ulster County REConnect will also encourage our residents to enjoy a healthy and active lifestyle by learning about and ultimately making continued use of the ample opportunities and assets that the county is so fortunate to have.

For additional information on any of the recreational features on the map, just double click on the icon and additional information will be displayed.

We hope that you will enjoy using Ulster County REConnect whether you are a resident of or visitor to our region. We encourage you get outdoors and explore our recreational resources firsthand... don't just take our word for it!

Ulster County REConnect evolved out of the production of a printed recreation map enclosed with the 2011 Ulster County Tourism Travel Guide. This initiative is a joint effort between Ulster County Department of Information Services and the Department of the Environment.

Ulster County REConnect is not possible without many agencies and organizations that graciously provided us with their data. Those groups are: Esopus Creek Conservancy, Finger Lakes Trail Conference, Frost Valley YMCA, Mohonk Preserve, New York / New Jersey Trail Conference, NYC Department of Environmental Protection, NYS Department of Environmental Conservation, NYS Office of Parks Recreation and Historic Preservation, Scenic Hudson, Ulster County Planning Department and the Ulster County Transportation Council.

Information contained herein is provided for informational purposes only. Ulster County provides geographic information systems (GIS) data with no claim as to the completeness, usefulness, or accuracy of its content, positional or otherwise.

The Ulster County REConnect application is designed to be used for the planning of recreational activities. The users should be aware of all rules and regulations relevant to recreational activities that are accessible through this application. Outdoor activities are potentially hazardous. There is always the possibility of personal injury or even death. It is the user's responsibility to understand and accept the risks involved and to take adequate precautions against them. Although care is taken to provide accurate information on UC RECconnect, this cannot ever be a substitute for thorough research, planning and judgement with regards to a particular outdoor activity.
